How to use

Manually enter each product from your paycheck. All prices are assumed to be in the globally selected currency (shown in the top-right corner). Dashboards will convert totals into that currency automatically.

  • Required fields: date, product, category, items quantity, price.
  • Quantity must be a positive integer.
  • Price must be a positive number (per single item).
